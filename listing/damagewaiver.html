How damage protection works

Our short-term rental damage plan will protect you while eliminating the requirement to collect and refund security deposits.

Your guest will pay a non-refundable fee of $1 per day, no less than $10 that is added to the total price during booking. This is intended for any minor damage caused during their stay at your rental property. Then, Crewdogs will reimburse you up to $1,000 in damages if disputes occur. See our full policy here

Exception: for official government travel stays, this amount is removed prior to your payout

It’s quick, it’s reliable, and we don’t outsource the reimbursement process.

Why Veterans created Crewdogs

  • Reduce Costs - only a 2% service fee let’s you pass the savings to guests
  • Trust For the Military Community - everyone on Crewdogs is verified by ID.me or a mil email
  • Support your Military Cause - we donate to it plus give you the option to as well
  • Government Travel Support - per diem rates, itemized receipts, and 24/7 support
  • Make Hosting Easier - connect pricing and calendar info from Airbnb, or use an iCal URL Parents

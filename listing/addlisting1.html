What we provide for official travel

  • Payouts after 30 days or check-out
  • Fees averaged into nightly rate
  • Automatic per diem calculations
  • GSA compliant itemized receipts
  • Exempting state taxes if needed

What you must provide for official travel

  • Fire safety compliance
  • Complimentary internet
  • Flexible cancellation policy
  • No late or early check-out fees

Don’t worry, we will follow up after the completion of your listing to ensure you are willing and able to host official travel.

The military discount

Your military discount is applied to the total nightly or monthly rate, not including fees or deposits. It is stacked on top of any length of stay discounts you apply (added on next page). We display this prominently on your listing for guests to see.

When the military discount is not applied:

Official gov’t travel stays where your rates have been reduced to reflect per diem amounts (if opted in for official gov’t hosting).

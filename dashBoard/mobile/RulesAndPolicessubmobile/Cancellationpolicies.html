Cancellation policies

Short-term: Flexible

Long-term: Standard

Short-term

Flexible

Full refund 1 day prior to arrival. If they cancel after that, you'll charge for each night they stay, plus 1 additional night and processing fees. This is auto applied policy for Gov't Travel stays.

Standard

Full refund 7 days prior to arrival. After that, 50% refund up to check-in. No refund after that.

Superstrict

Full refund for cancellations within 48 hours of booking and atleast 30 days before check-in. No refund after that.

Long-term

Applies for stays 28 nights to 6 months. After which, hosts can use discretion per the agreed upon lease agreement.

Standard

Full refund up to 30 days before arrival. After that, the first 30 days of the stay are non-refundable. This is not applied for stays of greater than 4 months. This should be covered in the lease agreement.
